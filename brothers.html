Brothers on the Hill

Post-apocalyptic survival narrative. Two brothers on an isolated hilltop during a zombie outbreak. The older brother must repeatedly descend into dangerous zones to scavenge food and supplies.

This project is currently in development. Details and media will be added as progress is made.
Read the Game Design Document >
Narrative Concept

The story is about responsibility and the cost of protection but I didn't want to explain that through cutscenes. Every scavenging run is the older brother leaving his younger brother behind to get food. The hilltop is safe. Leaving it isn't. That tension is the whole game.

Supplies connect directly to the younger brother's survival. It's not abstract resource management it's whether he eats tonight.

Planned Systems
  • Advanced Zombie AI (8-state FSM) as the core threat same system as my standalone project, but now the player is the one trying to stay quiet and get out.
  • Scavenging zones with NavMesh patrol patterns that get more aggressive the longer you stay.
  • Squad alerting as a central mechanic staying quiet has real consequences, not just a difficulty suggestion.
  • ZombieSoundSystem integration: the player's actions (footsteps, item pickup, gunshots) feed into the same sound detection bus the AI already uses.
  • Inventory tracking food and supplies per run, tied back to the shelter's state.
